Welcome to The Hammer Circle

Established in 1952, The Hammer Circle is the oldest specialist athletic technical club in the UK, dedicated to the promotion and development of hammer throwing.

Our members include athletes, coaches, and enthusiasts who share a passion for this unique and challenging event.
