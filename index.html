Welcome

Welcome to the Hammer Circle – the home of hammer throwing and the longest existing specialist event club in the UK. Founded in 1952, the Hammer Circle is a community of athletes, coaches, and supporters dedicated to the development and promotion of the hammer throw. Whether you're an experienced thrower, a newcomer to the sport, or simply passionate about athletics, the Hammer Circle offers a wealth of resources, events, and support to help you grow and connect with others. Join us in celebrating one of track and field’s most technical and powerful disciplines.
