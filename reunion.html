2025 HAMMER CIRCLE REUNION

The Hammer Circle is pleased to confirm that the 2025 Hammer Circle Reunion will take place on Saturday 27th and Sunday 28th September at Costello Stadium, Hull. Securing the date has taken careful planning this year, particularly to avoid clashes with the European Masters Championships, which are scheduled for mid-October and will involve a number of our members. With the track now officially booked, preparations can begin in earnest. As always, the Reunion will be a celebration of the past season’s achievements and an opportunity to bring together athletes, coaches, officials, and supporters for a weekend of competitions, coaching, and camaraderie. Further details about the event schedule, featured guests, competitions, and presentations will be announced in the coming months. We look forward to another memorable gathering that honours the tradition of the Circle and supports the future of hammer throwing in the UK. Mark your calendars and stay tuned for updates!
