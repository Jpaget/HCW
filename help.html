Hammer Circle Construction

This document provides comprehensive instruction on building a concentric hammer-discuss circle suitable for both hammer and discus in wet and dry conditions. This specification has been used for the construction of the circles of the 2012 London Olympics. This surface is also suitable for shot circles.
